What is NetApp Cloud Volumes ONTAP

Meet NetApp Cloud Volumes ONTAP, the solution that simplifies and integrates data management across cloud and on-premises, giving you the best of both environments. With its single, centralized management console and storage efficiency technologies, get ready to seamlessly manage your data across its entire lifecycle – fuelling your digital transformation.

With ONTAP Cloud, You Can:

Lift and shift your applications to the cloud

As efficient data management is critical to your operation, this solution gives you the protection, visibility, and control you need to seamlessly meet your application demands.

Accelerate your DevOps

Along with the storage resource efficiency on AWS, you are now able to instantaneously build, clone and tear down thousands of dev/test environments in minutes.

Save costs with storage tiering

By using EBS as a performance tier and AWS S3 as a capacity tier, you can automatically push your backups or infrequently accessed data to a more cost-effective storage – which is accessible anytime you need it.
